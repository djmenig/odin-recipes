Breakfast Quiche

Ham & Cheese Quiche with Sweet Potato Crust

This is a breakfast quiche that is a complete meal all in one. It combines the sweetness of the potato with eggs, cheese, onion, and red pepper.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 2/3 cups of egg whites
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • ~3.75 oz of hame steak cubed
  • 1/3 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled & grated

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Coat casserole or baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Peel & grate the sweet potato.
  4. Line the prepared baking disk with the sweet potato so that it resemembled a pie crust.
  5. Combine the chopped onion, chopped bell pepper, and cubed hame steak in a mixing bowl.
  6. Whisk together the eggs, egg whites, milk, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl; set aside.
  7. Transfer the dry ingredients into the prepared baking dish with the sweet potato crust.
  8. Pour the liquid ingredients on top of the dry ingredients.
  9. Bake for 60 minutes at 350F, or until quiche is not runny.
